DRIP COFFEE

 

Gather 4 tablespoons of coffee beans, 16 ounces of water, a coffee cone, paper filter and a coffee cup.

 

​1

Grind coffee. The ground should be soft and a little bit gritty.

 

​

​2

Pour coffee ground into filter to form a mound.

 

​

​3

Pour slowly in circular motion enough water so that the coffee will absorb it all but nothing will drip into your cup.

 

​

​4

Let it sit for 30-45 seconds. Pour again at a pace that will allow equal amounts of water to go into the grounds and through to your cup.

 

​

​5

Discard the filter and grounds and enjoy your piping hot cup of java.
