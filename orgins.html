A nice Guatamala Strictly Hard Bean (SHB) has a bright, clean acidity and a medium body, and sometimes just a hint of chocolate. It can take a lot of heat in the roasting, and fulfills a role in blends similar to Costa Rica SHB, mainly to give it the top-end bright acidity.

Kevin Keriuki believes that it is important to value the people who grow and harvest coffee cherries, the cultures that exist where coffees are cultivated and the economic relationships that are formed with every coffee purchase. He and his Koffee Kart baristas strive to connect with origin and support the communities that start their beans on their path to your cup.
